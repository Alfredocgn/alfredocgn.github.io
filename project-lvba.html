La vinícola Bs. As.

  • Print Design
  • Social Media
Visual identity and social media content for La Vinícola, a wine shop with over 1200 labels of mid-range and high-end wines. Located in Palermo, Buenos Aires, Argentina, it has another location in the City of Mendoza. They offer a wine-by-the-glass option accompanied by a selection of cheeses and cold cuts, allowing customers to stay and sample various wines. They also host weekly wine tastings and occasionally feature tastings with renowned sommeliers and winemakers.
